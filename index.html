Who Are we?

What is Ramadan? It's the most sacred month of the year in Islamic culture. Where millons of muslims around the world fast, abstain from pleasures, and pray intensely for most of the day. These traditions may sound like torture, but after sunset many families gather and celebrate. Now how can we celebreate without food? You guessed it. Muslims all over the world cook TONS of food during ramadan.

Much of the food that is cooked go to waste. Many people around the world face hungry daily and this is a perfect opportunity to help. Our mission is to help raise funds and donations to provide food to people who are less fortunate. More than 1.2 million New Yorkers face hunger every year. During this month of giving, why don't we muslims addiction non-muslims come together and help our community?
